About RESSS

Learn about our journey, mission, and the values that drive our community-focused investment approach.

Our Story

RESSS began as a weekly savings society in Gaibandha in 2013. What started as a small community initiative has now grown into a trusted investment platform serving hundreds of members across various sectors.

Our founder, recognizing the need for transparent and accessible investment opportunities in rural communities, established RESSS with the vision of creating sustainable financial growth for all members.

Over the years, we have expanded our portfolio to include seasonal crop financing, agricultural projects, land-backed cultivation loans, rental properties, and small business investments - always maintaining our commitment to community development and responsible investing.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through transparent, accessible investment opportunities that generate sustainable financial growth while prioritizing social impact and community development.

Our Vision

To become the leading community-focused investment platform in Bangladesh, known for transparency, reliability, and creating meaningful financial opportunities for all members regardless of their background.

Our Values

Transparency

We maintain open communication and clear records, ensuring all members have full visibility into our operations.

Community First

Our decisions prioritize the well-being of our members and the communities we serve above all else.

Sustainability

We focus on long-term growth and environmental responsibility in all our investment decisions.
