About RESSS

RESSS began as a weekly savings society in Gaibandha in 2014. Since then, we have expanded across a range of community-focused investment opportunities: seasonal Rabi crop financing, agro-farming projects, land-backed rice cultivation loans, and rental investments such as shop leases. Our model blends careful local knowledge with transparent processes to increase members' capital and weekly savings sustainably.
