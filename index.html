About RESSS

RESSS began as a weekly savings society in Gaibandha in 2013. Since then, we have expanded across a range of community-focused investment opportunities: seasonal Rabi crop financing, agro-farming projects, land-backed rice cultivation loans, and rental investments such as shop leases. Our model blends careful local knowledge with transparent processes to increase members' capital and weekly savings sustainably.

Over the years, we have built trust within our community by focusing on transparency and fairness. With hundreds of members contributing and benefitting from our initiatives, RESSS is now a cornerstone of responsible local investment and financial inclusion.
